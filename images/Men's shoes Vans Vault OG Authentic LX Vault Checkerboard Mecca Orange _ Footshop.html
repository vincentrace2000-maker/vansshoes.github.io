Privacy and cookie settings

Footshop a.s. uses cookies and personal data to ensure the reliability and security of its website, to monitor its performance and to personalise its content and the content of its advertisements to each customer. For more information, see our privacy policy.

The law states that we may only store and process data for which consent has been given or which is strictly necessary for the operation of this website.

 

These data include, in particular, identifiers and other data obtained or stored in cookies (small text files in your browser). This site uses different types of cookies. Some cookies are placed by third party services that appear on our site. We also pass some of your personal data to our partners for analytical and marketing purposes.

 

You can change or withdraw your consent via the "Consent settings/cookies" link in the footer of the site.

 

For more information, see our privacy policy.

Strictly necessary cookies are needed for the website to function properly, so it is not possible to disable them. They help to make a website usable by enabling basic functions such as site navigation, logging in, accessing secure sections of the website, etc. You can set your browser to block these files as well, but at the risk that the site may not work.

Preference cookies allow a website to remember information that changes how a website behaves or looks depending on your preferences and behaviour. This is for example your preferred language or region where you are located, personalised product listings, etc.

Analytical cookies help to track the number of visitors and from which sources they come. This helps us to understand what visitors are interested in (or uninterested in), how they move around the site, what elements they use, etc., so that we can improve the performance of our site.

Marketing cookies are used to track visitors to the website and our site and are set by the partners we use for marketing. These cookies allow them to create profiles about your interests and target advertising within their advertising systems (i.e. on other websites) accordingly. The intention is to show you advertising that is relevant and interesting to you and therefore more valuable to publishers and third-party advertisers.

 

At the same time, you consent to the transfer of your personal data (email, tel) entered in the forms to our advertising partners (Google, Facebook,...) for the purpose of tracking, targeting and customising advertising based on first party data, including linking the various tools used for analytics and marketing.

Vans Checkerboard

Vans Checkerboard - starring a checkerboard! Slip-Ons, Old Skooly, Sk8-Hi and other well-known and time-tested silhouettes in a checkerboard design. The roots of the use of this pattern on Vans date back to the late 70s when Steve Van Doren, son of founder Paul, noticed skaters colouring the white soles of the Slip-Ons with black marker to create that aforementioned checkered design. As with many other Vans models, the Checkerboard collection is based on the skate pop culture of the last century. The Checkerboards definitely became legendary after 1982, hand in hand with the premiere of the movie Golden Times at Ridgemont High, where they were worn by the film's main character, played by Sean Penn. Their clever two-tone design also makes them much easier to match with your outfit, so pick yours up here at Footshop!

Vans Authentic

The Vans Authentic shoe was the first model produced by Vans since its inception in 1966. It was designed by Paul Van Doren, the company's founder, and remains popular today in skateboarding, punk and many other cultures where it has earned a reputation as an iconic shoe.
